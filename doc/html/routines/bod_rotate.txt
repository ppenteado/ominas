BOD_ROTATE


 PURPOSE:
	Rotates a body about one its axes.


 CATEGORY:
	NV/LIB/BOD


 CALLING SEQUENCE:
	bod_rotate, bd, theta, axis=axis


 ARGUMENTS:
  INPUT: 
	bx:	 	Any subclass of BODY (nt).

	theta:		Angle of rotation (nt).

  OUTPUT: NONE


 KEYWORDS:
  INPUT: axis:		Body axis about which to rotate; default is 2.

  OUTPUT: NONE


 RETURN:
	NONE


 STATUS:
	Complete

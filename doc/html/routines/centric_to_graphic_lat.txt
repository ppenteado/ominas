CENTRIC_TO_GRAPHIC_LAT


 PURPOSE:
	Converts planetocentric latitudes to planetographic latitudes.


 CATEGORY:
	UTIL


 CALLING SEQUENCE:
	result = centric_to_graphic_lat(a, b, lat)


 ARGUMENTS:
  INPUT:
	a:	Polar radius.

	b:	Equatorial radius.

	lat:	Planetographic latitudes.

  OUTPUT: NONE


 KEYWORDS: NONE


 RETURN:
	Planetocentric latitudes.


 STATUS:
	Complete

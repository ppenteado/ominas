SURFACE_TO_DEGREES


 PURPOSE:
       Converts angular part of surface vectors from radians to degrees


 CATEGORY:
       NV/LIB/TOOLS


 CALLING SEQUENCE:
       result = surface_to_degrees(v)


 ARGUMENTS:
  INPUT:
              v:       An array of surface vectors (nv,3,nt).

  OUTPUT:
       NONE

 RETURN:
       Array of vectors (nv,3,nt)

 STATUS:
       Completed.
